About this space

A 2,400 sq ft open-air terrace perched on the 9th floor of a heritage building in Nungambakkam, with unobstructed views of the Chennai skyline. String lights, a built-in bar counter, and modular seating make this the go-to spot for sundowners, private dinners, and intimate gatherings.

The space comes fully dressed — you just bring the people. A dedicated event coordinator is on-site every booking.
